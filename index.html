Expanding Your Mind

In order to expand your mind you must first learn to quiet your mind...

Start here to begin this process:

  1. Sit down in a quiet space...
  2. Close your eyes and feel the floor underneath you...
  3. Take a deep breath in and hold for 10 seconds...
  4. Slowly breathe out for 10 seconds...
  5. Then slowly take another breath over 10 seconds...
  6. Repeat until you feel you feel the pressure in your body dissapate.
